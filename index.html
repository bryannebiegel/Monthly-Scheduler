DISTRIBUTION
How assignments are decided

Hard rules (never broken): a person is never scheduled on a requested day off, never exceeds their days-per-week target in any calendar week, and never exceeds their max-consecutive-days cap. Within those limits the engine balances total shifts so everyone gets roughly even hours, and fills each shift block up to your coverage target. When there aren't enough eligible people to hit coverage, the day is flagged amber/red so you can adjust manually.
